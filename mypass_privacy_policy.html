Privacy Policy for VaultDog

Last updated: January 29, 2025

VaultDog is developed and maintained by NOAGAGIN. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our password management application.

Simple Truth: We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

1. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

VaultDog operates completely offline. All your data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • No cloud synchronization
  • No remote servers
  • No data transmission to any external services
  • No backup to our servers

Encryption

Your data is protected using strong encryption:

  • All stored data is encrypted using your master password as the encryption key
  • Your master password is never stored anywhere, not even on your device
  • Exported backup files are encrypted with the same master password
  • Without your master password, the data cannot be decrypted by anyone, including us
CRITICAL: If you forget your master password, there is no way to recover your data. We cannot help you reset or recover it because we never have access to it. Keep your master password safe and memorable.

2. What We DO NOT Collect

VaultDog does not collect, transmit, or process any of the following:

  • Personal Information: No names, emails, phone numbers, or addresses
  • Passwords: We never see, store, or transmit your passwords or master password
  • Usage Data: No analytics, tracking, or telemetry
  • Device Information: No device IDs, IP addresses, or hardware information
  • Location Data: No location tracking of any kind
  • Crash Reports: No automatic crash reporting or error logging

3. Internet Connection

VaultDog does not require or use internet connection:

  • The app functions completely offline
  • No data is sent to or received from any servers
  • No third-party services are integrated

4. Biometric Authentication

If you choose to enable biometric authentication (fingerprint or face unlock):

  • Biometric data is processed entirely by your device's operating system
  • VaultDog never accesses, stores, or transmits your biometric information
  • Biometric authentication is optional and can be disabled at any time
  • It serves only as a convenience feature and does not replace your master password

5. Import and Export

Export

When you export your data:

  • The exported file is encrypted using your master password
  • The file remains on your device unless you manually move it
  • We have no access to exported files

Import

When you import data:

  • The file is read and decrypted locally on your device
  • No data is sent anywhere during the import process

6. Third-Party Services

VaultDog does not integrate with any third-party services:

  • No advertisements
  • No analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • No social media integrations
  • No external SDKs or libraries that collect data

7. Children's Privacy

VaultDog does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. Since the app operates entirely offline with no data collection, it is safe for users of all ages.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have complete control over your data at all times
  • To delete your data, simply uninstall the app or clear its data through your device settings
  • We retain no data because we never collect any

9. Your Responsibilities

As a user of VaultDog, you are responsible for:

  • Keeping your master password secure and memorable
  • Creating encrypted backups if you want to preserve your data
  • Understanding that forgotten passwords cannot be recovered
  • Protecting your device with appropriate security measures

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Since VaultDog does not collect data, changes to this policy are unlikely to affect your privacy in any meaningful way.

11. Permissions

VaultDog requires minimal permissions:

  • Storage: To save and read encrypted data files on your device
  • Biometric (optional): To enable fingerprint/face unlock if you choose

These permissions are used exclusively for the app's core functionality and nothing else.
